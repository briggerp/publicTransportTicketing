Understanding Your Options

  • Tram Abo: Yearly tram subscription (586 CHF youth/809 CHF adult) - train trips paid at full price
  • Half-Tax (HT): Basic discount card (100 CHF youth/170 CHF adult) - 50% off train trips
  • GA Night: Night travel card for youth (100 CHF, under 25 only)
  • Tram Abo + HT Plus: Yearly tram subscription combined with train discount and bonus credit
  • HT Plus: Train and tram discount card with credit bonus
  • GA: General Abonnement - unlimited travel on all public transport
  • Bonus: Remaining credit from HT Plus cards that can be used for future trips
